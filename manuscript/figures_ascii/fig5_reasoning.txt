   Clustering rule:
     group by (assertion_type, addresses_concern)
     within each group, single-link union-find on
       cosine( BGE(assertion_text), BGE(other_text) ) ≥ 0.75

   Resolution rule  (only when len(cluster) > 1 AND texts disagree):
     1. recency  →  2. authority  →  3. evidence_strength
     →  4. coverage  →  5. confidence
     (first rule that produces a unique winner stops the chain;
      every non-primary assertion is retained as an alternative
      with retain_reason — the prompt forbids dropping any)
